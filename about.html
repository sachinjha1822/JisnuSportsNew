ABOUT JISNU

Sports Promotion and Support: JisnuSports focuses on promoting sports through donations, events, and community involvement, fostering a culture of support for the sports community.

Transparent Donation Tracking: The platform ensures transparent tracking of donations, documenting donor details, amounts, contact information, and payment methods for accountability.

Monthly Pass System: Subscribers can contribute a minimum of ₹50 through a monthly pass, supporting ongoing sports initiatives and events.

Expense and Event Management: JisnuSports tracks expenses for sports activities and highlights upcoming events to keep the community informed and engaged.

Engaging Social Media Presence: JisnuSports maintains active Instagram and YouTube channels to engage sports enthusiasts, share insights, and drive community participation in supporting sports organizations and causes.

About us

JisnuSports is more than just a sports platform—it’s a movement dedicated to empowering athletes, supporting underprivileged sports communities, and promoting the spirit of sportsmanship. We believe that every athlete deserves a chance to shine, regardless of their background or financial status.
